Our Mission

Our mission is to build on the skills already taught to students and provide a sense of community to Red River College's Technology students by improving relationships between current students, instructors, industry and alumni. We accomplish this by providing volunteer opportunities, information/education sessions and social events.

Our Story

The Bits and Bytes Association (BBA) was created to improve communication between students and staff to enhance the quality of education for Introduction to Business Information Technology (IBIT), Business Information Technology (BIT) and Business Technology Management (BTM) programs offered at Red River College through the Applied Computer Education (ACE) department. Since its inception in 2015, BBA has grown to over 100 members today and continues to climb steadily.

Get Involved

Participation in the Bits and Bytes Association is proven to increase the skills required to perform in industry today. However, we understand that there are other commitments, assignments and the need for personal time. Joining us is optional and you are welcome to engage as much or as little as you want.

  • Come to our general meetings
  • Join the discussions on social media
  • Stop by our events (or plan one!)
  • Volunteer! - We always have new opportunities available
  • Celebrate finishing exams with us
  • Contribute to our website
